We utilize inputs from this vast reservoir of talents as and when required.

About Us

Here at Jetfix Autocare we value our Customers and their opinions. We understand that customers have a vast list of options as to where to go for Automobile wash and Auto repairs. Jetfix Autocare is committed to helping car owners in and outside of Kwara state maintain their vehicles and to do so with honesty and integrity. With our state-of-the-art equipment, we take care of all automobile repairs, maintenance and tyre needs. From oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid flushes to brake repairs, shocks absorber, tyres, wheel balancing and alignment, name it we've got you covered.

Our Vision

To be abreast on all automobile technologies which will aid us to offer reliable automotive services and repairs. We also strive to develop an unmatched reputation with our customers within the community in terms of quality of service and delivery.

Our Mission

To provide the best of automobile care, quality workmanship and only the most exceptional of services. We are dedicated to staying committed to Our customers. Jetfix Autocare offers a clean and comfortable environment for our customers, where we strive to surpass our customers' expectations in every way, and to guarantee all repairs a job well done.
